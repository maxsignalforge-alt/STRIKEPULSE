Users are responsible for their own decisions, risk controls, order entry, gains, losses, taxes, and compliance with applicable laws and broker requirements.

Do not enter brokerage credentials, payment card data, government IDs, private account numbers, or sensitive personal information into prototype fields.

Market data may be delayed, incomplete, plan-limited, rate-limited, simulated, or unavailable. Live market-data redistribution requires provider licensing before public paid launch.

These terms are a product-readiness draft and should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before commercial launch.
