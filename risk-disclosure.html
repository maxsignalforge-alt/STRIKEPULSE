Risk Disclosure

Prototype draft. Last updated June 2, 2026.

Trading involves substantial risk. Options are complex, leveraged instruments and can expire worthless. Losses can occur quickly and may exceed expectations.

STRIKEPULSE scores, alerts, charts, market context, AI summaries, and trade tickets do not guarantee profitable outcomes. A high-confidence setup can still lose money.

Market data can be delayed, incomplete, rate-limited, plan-blocked, stale, or incorrect. News, earnings, economic events, liquidity, volatility, spreads, and broker execution can materially affect results.

Paper Trading is simulated. Simulated fills may not reflect bid/ask spreads, slippage, partial fills, commissions, fees, taxes, assignment, exercise, margin requirements, market halts, or order rejections.

Risk management rules such as stop prices, position sizing, reward/risk thresholds, and rejection logic are workflow aids. They are not guarantees of loss prevention.

Users should understand any instrument before trading it and should consult qualified financial, tax, or legal professionals where appropriate.

This disclosure is a product-readiness draft and should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before commercial launch.
