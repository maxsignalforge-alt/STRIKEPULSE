How Scores Work

Transparent rules for decision support. These scores do not predict or guarantee outcomes.

Quality Gate

Combines signal confidence, entry timing, options liquidity, reward/risk, market context, IV risk, expiry risk, moneyness risk, and 9-Sig confluence.

Hard skips can happen when contracts are too thin, reward/risk is too weak, or the ticker is fighting the broader tape.

9-Sig Confluence

Checks nine conditions: signal, entry, contract, spread, reward/risk, IV, tape, breadth, and VIX.

A high score means more conditions are aligned. It does not mean the trade will win.

Contract Selector

Prefers contracts with cleaner liquidity, reasonable spread, useful delta, and manageable theta. 0DTE and OTM contracts are treated as more aggressive.

Practice Grade

Grades process quality at entry: Quality Gate, entry status, 9-Sig, liquidity, and reward/risk. Profit alone does not create an A-grade trade.
